# Sound Files for IPL Auction

Place the following sound files in this folder:

1. **countdown.mp3** - Countdown timer tick sound (plays in last 5 seconds)
2. **bid.wav** - Short sound when a bid is placed
3. **sold.mp3** - Sound when player is sold
4. **unsold.mp3** - Sound when player goes unsold

## Where to get sounds:

You can download free sound effects from:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

## Recommended sounds:

- **countdown.mp3**: Clock tick or beep sound
- **bid.wav**: Cash register or bell sound
- **sold.mp3**: Success chime or applause
- **unsold.mp3**: Buzzer or negative sound

For now, the app will work without sounds (they're optional).
